# Tojo
Control two players at the same time, switch gravity, and solve unique puzzles.

Welcome to a unique 2D puzzle adventure where two players move simultaneously, each in their own world with different gravity rules. Your mission is to help them find their way to each other!

Use gravity switching, avoid traps, disable lasers, press buttons, open doors, and discover paths that lead both characters to the shared exit point.

Features:

⭐ Control two characters at the same time
⭐ Two mirrored worlds with different gravity
⭐ Challenging levels and clever puzzles
⭐ Buttons, doors, lasers, and more interactive mechanics
⭐ Simple controls and intuitive gameplay
⭐ Atmospheric minimalist art style
⭐ Your progress is saved automatically

Help the heroes overcome all obstacles and reunite!
